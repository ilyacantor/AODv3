**Why this is happening (plain English)**
Nothing is “wrong” or missing by accident. In this iteration you **correctly removed pre-adjudicated concepts** (shadow, zombie) from the pipeline. Because of that:

* **Shadow** used to come from Farm labels or shortcut logic
* **Zombie** used to come from lifecycle heuristics mixed into admission
* In AOD Fresh, neither is derived yet from **evidence-only rules**, so they don’t appear

This is actually a **sign of architectural health**, not regression.

Right now AOD produces:

* assets
* findings
* gaps / conflicts

…but it does **not yet compose higher-order states** like *shadow* or *zombie*.

---

**What to do next (one iteration only)**
Add **derived classifications** for *shadow* and *zombie* as **post-processing views**, not ingestion concepts.

They should be:

* computed from existing evidence + findings
* explainable
* optional (views), not baked into core pipeline
