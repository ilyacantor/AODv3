Questions for AOD Team
Based on Farm-side analysis of reconciliation discrepancies, here are the questions that need clarification:

1. Multi-Domain Vendor Normalization
Context: Farm normalizes googleapis.com, gstatic.com, googleusercontent.com → google.com (parent consolidation). Similarly for Microsoft: office.com, sharepoint.com → microsoft.com.

Questions:

Does AOD consolidate multi-domain vendor aliases to their parent domain, or keep them as separate catalog entries?
If AOD keeps them separate, what is the canonical key format? (e.g., googleapis.com vs Google Workspace - googleapis.com)
Should Farm disable parent consolidation for MULTI_DOMAIN_PRODUCTS to match AOD behavior?
2. Zombie Detection Logic
Context: Farm expects 25 zombies, AOD found only 4 (84% miss rate).

Questions:

What is AOD's zombie_window_days setting? (Farm uses 90 days)
How does AOD determine "last activity timestamp"? Does it use:
Last SSO event?
Last finance transaction?
Last discovery observation?
All of the above (max)?
Are zombies only detected for governed assets (in CMDB/IdP), or also for ungoverned assets with stale activity?
Does AOD require minimum evidence thresholds before classifying as zombie (e.g., must have CMDB entry)?
3. Synthetic Domain Passthrough
Context: 21 synthetic SaaS domains (linkbox.dev, flexbase.co, workbox.app, etc.) show as KEY_NORMALIZATION_BUG.

Questions:

Does AOD apply any domain filtering/exclusions beyond the policy exclusions list?
Are there minimum observation thresholds (e.g., discovery_source_count >= 2) that reject single-source synthetic domains?
Does AOD strip any TLD patterns (.dev, .app, .tech) as non-production?
4. Admission Gate Alignment
Context: 72 assets rejected by AOD that Farm expected to admit.

Questions:

What are AOD's minimum admission criteria? Farm uses:
In CMDB → admit
In IdP → admit
Has discovery + (cloud evidence OR finance spend > $0) → admit
Does AOD have additional gates (e.g., minimum user count, activity recency)?
5. False Positive Shadows (29 assets)
Context: Farm classified as clean/governed but AOD says shadow.

Questions:

How does AOD determine governance status? Farm uses:
governed = in_cmdb OR in_idp
Does AOD require both CMDB and IdP for full governance, or just one?
Are there CMDB lifecycle states that AOD treats as "ungoverned" (e.g., pending, draft)?
6. Policy Snapshot Verification
Questions:

Does AOD include policy_snapshot in its run output so Farm can verify grading against the exact policy used?
If policy differs between Farm and AOD, which takes precedence for reconciliation?
These questions will help identify whether discrepancies are Farm bugs (Farm should change) or expected differences (AOD behavior is correct and Farm needs alignment)
