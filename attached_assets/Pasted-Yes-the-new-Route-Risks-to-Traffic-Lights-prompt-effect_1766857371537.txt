🚜 Master Prompt: Implement Risk Routing & Toxic Assets
Context: We are finalizing the Triage logic. We must ensure that specific Risk Findings are routed to the correct "Traffic Light" queue. We are NOT creating a 4th "Security Risk" category.

Task: Update findings_engine.py and the Triage UI logic to implement this routing table.

1. The "Toxic Asset" Logic (Yellow Lane)

Definition: An asset is Toxic if provisioning_status == 'ACTIVE' AND it has a IDENTITY_GAP (Missing from IdP).

Routing: Force these assets into the 🟡 Risk & Review Queue (Do not leave them in Green).

UI Actions:

For Toxic Assets, show a "Dismiss Risk" button.

Logic: This keeps the asset ACTIVE but suppresses the IDENTITY_GAP finding for future runs (effectively "accepting" the risk of password-only login).

Note: "Deprovision" should also be available (standard behavior).

2. The "High-Value Shadow" Logic (Red Lane)

Definition: An asset is Shadow if provisioning_status == 'QUARANTINE'.

Routing: Ensure FINANCE_GAP (Spend > $0) findings are attached here.

Visual Priority: In the 🔴 Blocked Assets Queue, sort assets with FINANCE_GAP to the top. Use a ($) badge or similar indicator to show money is burning.

3. The "Hygiene" Logic (Green Lane)

Definition: DATA_CONFLICT or GOVERNANCE_GAP (Missing Owner).

Routing: Keep these in the 🟢 Governance Gaps Queue.

Action: Standard ACKNOWLEDGE or ASSIGN OWNER.

Scenario A (Toxic): Create an Active Asset (CMDB=True) with No IdP.
